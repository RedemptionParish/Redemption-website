About Us

The Redeemed Christian Church of God, Redemption Parish was founded in the early 2000's under LP17. It was then pastored by Pastor Oriyomi and with time it grew into an Area headquarter which was pioneered by Pastor Oluwalanke before turning into a zonal headquarter under the supervision of Pastor Amujesuniyi Ajayi. During that period, LP50 was created then it was moved from LP17 to LP50.
